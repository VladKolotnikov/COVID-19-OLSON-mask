About This Project

COVID-19 is one of the biggest challenge we are having to face today. With thousands dying world wide, according to the statistics gathered by ECDC news out of 150,000 deaths, 34,000 happened in America. The U.S. is currently in the lead both in number of infected, and deaths from coronovirus. This has to stop, and the only way we can stop it is by being ready for it. This is why people worldwide are banding together to create protection from this desease. We created this website to spread our cause. Every mask or every cent you can donate to the cause will not only protect those who can't afford safety from the pandemic, but also yourself, for we wil only be truly safe, when the virus is no more. This project is a call to protect yourself by protecting others, and together we will build a future without the virus. One mask at a time!

What We Do

In order to help stop this pandemic we are creating Olson masks. Even though they might not be as effective as the manufactured ones, they are much safer then the classical ones, hankerchiefs, bandanas, scarfs and etc. If everyone wears on of these mask it will prevnt the spread of the virus and will be a major contribution to the effort to end coronavirus once and for all.
