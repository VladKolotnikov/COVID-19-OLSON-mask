What We Do

This virus is a problem that affects all of us, and that is precisely why we need to band together to solve this crisis. That is why we started this project. We hand make masks to give them out to hospitals and doctors who fight on the front of this war for survival, as well as to those. Who can not afford to make or buy these masks. Every single mask saves the person wearing it, and those around him, which is why it is vital everyone has access to it. We would greatly appreciate volunteers to take this project up in their areas by creating and providing masks to people living around. We also graciously accept donations. 100% of the money we gather goes to buying supplies for masks and supporting hospitals in any way we can. Together we will stop this pandemic. One mask at a time!
